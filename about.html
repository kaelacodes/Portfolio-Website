How I Got Here

For many years now I have workied in the healthcare feild as a Certified Nursing Assistant, and most recently as a Hospice Aide, providing assistance with activities of daily living to my local elderly population. Having followed in my mothers footsteps I assumed that I would one day become a nurse just as she had in order to provide a more financially stable life for myself, though I always felt that it wasn't my right path.
After a converstaion with a close friend and colleague who was considering a career change into data analysis, I relaized for the first time that persuing a career in tech was not only possible for me, but was also a very attractive option. I began to explore the world of web development, and quickly fell in love finding that this world was one that clicked with my logical brain. Fast forward, and here I am persuing a radical change in career and looking forward to every twist and turn this new path make take me through.
